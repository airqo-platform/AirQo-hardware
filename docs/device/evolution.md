# Hardware Evolution

6 generations and counting — what started out as a simple breadboard prototype has evolved through the years. Here we take a look at the evolution of AirQo hardware.

---

## Hardware Generations

| Generation | Key Milestone |
|---|---|
| **Gen 1** | Initial breadboard prototype — proof of concept |
| **Gen 2** | First PCB design, basic PM2.5 sensing |
| **Gen 3** | Added GSM connectivity and cloud data upload |
| **Gen 4** | Solar power integration, improved enclosure |
| **Gen 5** | ATMEGA2560-based design, multi-sensor, LoRaWAN support |
| **Gen 6+** | Ongoing improvements in accuracy, power efficiency, and connectivity |

---

## Design Files

Hardware design files for current and previous generations are available in the [hardware folder](https://github.com/airqo-platform/AirQo-hardware/tree/main/hardware) of the repository, including:

- PCB schematics (KiCad)
- Gerber files for PCB manufacturing
- 3D enclosure designs for 3D printing
